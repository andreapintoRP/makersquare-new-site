Full Curriculum

We start where courses end.

No beginner hand-holding. No visual automation tools. Production prompting, RAG, agents, evals, and real deployment. This curriculum evolves with the industry.

Pre-Work

Three things before Day 1.

Self-paced. Complete before your admissions interview. This isn't busywork. Your pre-work is reviewed by the admissions team.

01

Tell us who you are

Describe your role, your industry, and a real problem you've tried to solve. The more specific, the better.

02

Pick your use case

Identify one task in your work that could be automated or improved with AI. Be specific: what goes in, what comes out, who uses it.

03

Build your first prompt system

Write a system prompt, test it on 5 real inputs, iterate, and submit your final prompt + 3 best outputs + notes on what broke.
